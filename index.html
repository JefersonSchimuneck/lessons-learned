I joined Trybe Class 08 on September/2020, looking for a career change, I had no previously coding experience. Even though I’m here for just a few weeks, I was able to learn a lot of technical skills! Trybe teaching methods focus on practical examples, to simulate situations we will face in a real work environment. They are also worried about our social abilities, so we are constantly developing our soft skills. For the future I hope to keep growing as a developer and as a person together with Trybe and my classmates.

"Personally, I was worried about the socialization moments, but actually they are way nicer than I thought! I met a lot of cool people and had some good laughs".
